Bugatti Chiron

Top Speed: 261 mph (420 km/h)

Engine: 8.0 L Quad-Turbocharged W16

The Bugatti Chiron is a mid-engine two-seater sports car developed and manufactured by French automotive marque Bugatti. It is the successor to the Bugatti Veyron, featuring a quad-turbocharged W16 engine and a top speed electronically limited to 261 mph.
